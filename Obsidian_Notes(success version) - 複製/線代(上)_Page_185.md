> **例 4**
>
> Suppose that $\begin{vmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{vmatrix} = 5$.
>
> (1) $\begin{vmatrix} 2a & 2b & 2c \\ 4g+3a & 4h+3b & 4i+3c \\ 3d-a & 3e-b & 3f-c \end{vmatrix} = ?$
>
> $\oplus \det\begin{bmatrix} 4a+5d+6g & 4b+5e+6h & 4c+5f+6i \\ 2a+3d & 2b+3e & 2c+3f \\ a & b & c \end{bmatrix} = ?$
>
> 【104 台南臺工商】

**解**

(1) $\begin{vmatrix} 2a & 2b & 2c \\ 4g+3a & 4h+3b & 4i+3c \\ 3d-a & 3e-b & 3f-c \end{vmatrix}$

$= 2 \times 4 \times 3 \times \begin{vmatrix} a & b & c \\ g & h & i \\ d & e & f \end{vmatrix}$

$= -24 \begin{vmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{vmatrix} = -24 \times 5 = -120$

(2) 可依數列改如下：

$$\begin{vmatrix} 4a+5d+6g & 4b+5e+6h & 4c+5f+6i \\ 2a+3d & 2b+3e & 2c+3f \\ a & b & c \end{vmatrix} = \begin{vmatrix} 4 & 5 & 0 \\ 2 & 3 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} \begin{vmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{vmatrix} = -18 \cdot 5 = -90$$
