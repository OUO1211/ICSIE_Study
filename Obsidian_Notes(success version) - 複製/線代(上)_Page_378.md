> **例題 22**
>
> (10%) Let $X_1$ and $X_2$ be 2-dimensional subspaces of $\mathbb{R}^3$. If $X_1 \ne X_2$ and $X_1 \cap X_2 \ne \{0\}$，find $\dim(X_1 + X_2)$.

**解** 3.

因 $X_1 \cap X_2 \subseteq X_1$，

但 $X_1 \ne X_2$，$\therefore X_1 \cap X_2 \ne X_1$，

$\therefore \dim(X_1 \cap X_2) < \dim(X_1) = 2$，

又因為 $X_1 \cap X_2 \ne \{0\}$，$\therefore \dim(X_1 \cap X_2) \ge 1$，

$\therefore \dim(X_1 \cap X_2) = 1$，

$\therefore \dim(X_1 + X_2) = \dim(X_1) + \dim(X_2) - \dim(X_1 \cap X_2) = 2 + 2 - 1 = 3$。
